Sweet Facts About Cotton Candy

Cotton candy, also known as "fairy floss," has been a beloved treat at fairs and carnivals since its invention in 1897.

Fun Fact: The first cotton candy machine was a joint invention by dentist William Morrison and confectioner John C. Wharton!
